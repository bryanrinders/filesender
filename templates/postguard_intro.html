Prototype: Filesender + PostGuard

This is a prototype, integrating PostGuard (PG) into Filesender. PG is an encryption service that is privacy- and user-friendly. Using PG, uploaders nolonger have to manually distribute the encryption password to the recipients. To use PG you'll need to download Yivi on your smartphone. For more details see the thesis I wrote about this.

Note

  • IMPORTANT: This prototype is provided without a logging in mechanism and hence every upload is visible to everyone and decryptable by anyone, so do not upload personal things.
  • PostGuard only works when uploading normal files, not archives such as zip or tar.
  • The prototype uses demo Yivi attributes that can be retrieved by anyone. Simply scan the QR code with your Yivi app and follow the instructions on your screen. This is also true for the email addresses of the recipients.
